Retail Sales Power BI Dashboard
Project Overview: This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using a slightly dirty retail sales dataset. The goal is to analyze business performance through key metrics such as total sales, profit margin, and average sales per invoice while identifying trends across regions, states, products, and sales channels.

Objectives:
Analyze overall retail sales performance
Identify high-performing and underperforming regions
Track monthly and quarterly sales trends
Evaluate profitability across products and sales channels
Generate insights to support business decision-making

Key KPIs:
Total Sales
Total Profit
Profit Margin %
Average Sales per Invoice

Dashboard Insights:
Sales trend analysis by month
Profit comparison by region
State-wise profit performance
Product revenue vs profit analysis
Sales channel profitability
Discount impact on profit margin

Tools Used:
Power BI
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Data Cleaning Techniques

Dataset: The dataset used in this project is Slightly Dirty Retail Sales Data, containing sales transactions, product details, regions, states, discount percentages, and profit information.

Outcome: The dashboard helps identify revenue patterns, profitability issues, and growth opportunities by visualizing key business metrics in an interactive format.
